A bit about me

Hi, I'm Emily and this website is all about ME!

I currently live in North London but am about to move back to my hometown of Northampton.

I'm a mother of two gorgeous boys aged 3 and 1.

I love learning, and have taught myself things like baking, chocolate-making, and now coding (which I'm finding utterly addictive!)

Why I want to learn to code

Whilst I love being a mum I want to start a career.

Having originally studied Philosophy and read an MA in Human Rights, I went on to do a Law Conversion Course with a view to beocming a Barrister in Public Law. I took my law exams whilst my eldest son was 3 months old, this was one of the hardest things I've ever done and involved studying during his naps and late into the night. My hard work paid off and I received a distinction.

I then worked as a volunteer in a Law Centre. I loved this work, especially the problem-solving it required, but it became increasingly apparent to me that the work I wanted to do was not going to be conducive with my family-life. I therefore had a rethink...

a long, hard, think

...before it hit me! My perfect career choice - CODING!

Why I think I'll be good at coding

  • I love a challenge
